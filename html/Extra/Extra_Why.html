Why Data?

My personal motives


A holistic vision of human happiness

While I chose engineering as my undergraduate major, I've always had profound interest in understanding the whole - what makes humans thrive and work together. I'm particularly passionate about families and rearing the next generation in a healthy way - and data is the best way to separate our false intuitions from what's really worth pursuing.
